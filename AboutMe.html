About Me

This was an exciting opportunity to surround myself with beautifully made, exclusive garments and help customers find the perfect garment for any occasion. This is undoubtably where I gained the most knowledge on sales and customer experience through first hand interactions with various clients.

While working at HSC I met an inspiring coworker who was studying a Bachelors in Consumer Sciences and as I was taking Consumer Sciences and Design in High School this seemed like a natural degree to pursue. The degree was a perfect blend of Business, Marketing and Design which aligned with my interests and experience. In this time I was also able to secure several part time employment opportunities in Marketing and as a Brand Ambassador.

With many friends in the IT field, I was reminded of the many bariers to my curiosity with computers growing up and realised that I could now work and fund a degree through UNISA to explore the fascinating world of computing. While it was certaily not an easy situation to be in while studying any degree, I am glad to have completed it.

Currently, I am based in Cape Town working remoterly as a Quadient Consultant for Beehive Online Solutions.
